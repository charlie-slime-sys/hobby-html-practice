The Delve Into Art

In the entirety of my life, art has always had my attention. Art can come in many, many different forms.

Art can be music, drawing, acting, and many other things.

Personally, I am into music and drawing.

What Can Art Benefit?

Art can benefit various factors:

  • Mental Health: Art and music are types of therapy. They can improve the mental state and cognitive skill for your imagination.
  • Imagination: Imagination is boosted through how one views things. Translating interpretation into something visual can make someone's theoretical skills improved, therefore letting out someone's enclosed thoughts.

These factors can also improve someone's work ethic. As making something fun - i.e. art - can provide motivation for other things. For example, math, or writing.

Ways To Improve Art, Even If You're "Bad" At It.

  1. Practice EVERY day, with any supplies you have. If you run out of motivation, put it down. It'll burn you out if you continue.
  2. Upgrade your materials. For traditional art, try to avoid starter kits and resort to buying specific supplies. This can also go the same way with musicianship.
  3. Experiment with style. If you never do this, you'll never know your capabilities with art, in any form.
  4. Along with style experimentation, experiment with technique. Technique will improve your style.
  5. Move out of simplicity. Practicing one thing over and over in technique never fully tests the technique. For instance, with art, if your style is cartoony or anime-esc, try realism. It will improve your skill with proportions.

With this information, it should be easy to become an intermediate artist of any kind. I encourage passion and effort into everything you do.
